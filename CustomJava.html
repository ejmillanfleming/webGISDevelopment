Fun Facts about Canada!

1. The highest mountain in Canada is Mount Logan with 5,959 m/ 19,551 ft. Mount Logan is located in the Yukon Territory at the border with Alaska/USA.

2. Canada has the highest tides in the world in the Bay of Fundy (Between Nova Scotia and New Brunswick). Tides can change up to 40 feet, every 7 hours!

3. A pole of inaccessibility is the most diffiuclt place to reach in a given region. This can be because of lack of roads, or distance from oceans. Canada's pole of inacessibility is located in Alberta at the Jackfish River.
